Daniel Goleman, Resilience for the Rest of Us, Harvard Business Review

 

Source: https://hbr.org/2011/04/resilience-for-the-rest-of-us

 

There are two ways to become more resilient: one by talking to yourself, the other by retraining your brain.

If you have sufferred a major failure:

Talk to yourself. Give yourself a cognitive intervention and counter defeatist thinking with an optimistic attitude. Challenge your downbeat thinking and replace it with a positive outlook.

What about bouncing back from the more frequent annoying screwups, minor setbacks, and irritating upsets?

Practice mindfulness mediation.
