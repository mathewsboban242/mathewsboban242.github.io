Mathews Boban

I am interested in probability, analysis, geometry, and theory of computation. I am currently interested in asymptotic convex geometry (the KLS conjecture, stochastic localization), functional analysis, isoperimetry, metric geometry (metric embeddings, the Ribe program), and Brownian motion (and it's applications in analysis of boolean functions; see for example, here and here.). I would also like to study functional inequalities such as Poincaré, log–Sobolev, and Sobolev inequalities for Markov semigroups.
I have worked in harmonic analysis of boolean functions as a research intern with Yuval Filmus from Nov. 2021 to Aug. 2022.
