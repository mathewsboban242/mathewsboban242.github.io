  • Actually on the first day when I came to meet with Benny [Sudakov], I thought it was a chat.. when I started as an official PhD student at Princeton. Three hours later we started talking, and he said, "Great, now tonight, why don't you do this? And we meet again tomorrow"... I did not know this is what would happen on the first day..
    ...so after I started to work with Benny, I discovered what I had gotten into.. So much so that one summer when I was working at a hedge fund..
