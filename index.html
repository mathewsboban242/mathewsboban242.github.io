Mathews Boban

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Washington, where I am fortunate to be advised by Thomas Rothvoss. I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science. Previously, I was a research assistant at the National University of Singapore with Arnab Bhattacharyya, Michael Choi, and Rahul Jain. Before that, I was a research assistant at the Technion with Yuval Filmus. Even before that, I was an undergraduate in computer science at IIT Bombay.
